about

hello.

my name is Dean Taylor.

i'm a berlin-based designer, creative technologist and musician.

in 2019, i founded an interactive art and design studio called eastlab. here we are dealing with interactive design and installations.

my relationship with design was born from my love of technology, the digital world, and physical form. honestly though, I can’t deny that sci-fi movies and techno had a part to play in it!

i am passionate about bringing the digital world into a physical context in order to create stronger bonds with technology. i believe good communication is key to creating strong relationships with people, and i believe this isn't unlike our behaviour towards design and technology.

if you have any questions or would like to hire me, please contact me at: me@deantaylordesign.com
